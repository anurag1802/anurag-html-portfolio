Frequently Asked Questions
  • 1. What types of graphic design services do you offer?
    We offer a wide range of graphic design services, including logo design, brochure design, infographics, social media graphics, website design, banner ads, and more.
  • 2.How do you work with clients to understand their needs and preferences?
    We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and preferences. We start with an initial consultation to discuss the project and gather information about the client's business and their target audience. We also provide regular updates and feedback throughout the design process to ensure that the final product meets the client's expectations.
  • 3.How long does it take to complete a graphic design project?
    The timeline for a graphic design project can vary depending on the complexity and scope of the project. We provide a detailed timeline and schedule during the initial consultation and keep our clients informed of any changes or delays.
  • 4. Can you provide revisions for the design?
    Yes, we provide revisions for the design. We work closely with the client to ensure that the final product meets their expectations and we will make any necessary revisions until the client is completely satisfied with the final design.
  • 5. What is your policy on copyright?
    We respect the copyright of all images and graphics used in our designs. We only use royalty-free images and graphics, and we will never use any copyrighted materials without the express permission of the owner. Additionally, we transfer all the rights of the design to the client once the project is completed and payment is made.
