Frequently Asked Questions
  • 1. How much do you charge for a website?
    The cost of website development can vary greatly depending on a number of factors such as the complexity of the website, the technologies used, and the experience level of the development team. Generally, It starts with ₹5k for basic website(Single page) and go upto more than ₹50k for E-commerce website.
  • 2. How long does it take to build a website?
    The amount of time it takes for website development can vary greatly depending on the complexity and size of the project. Factors such as the number of pages, functionality, and design elements can all impact the development time. On average, a basic website can take anywhere from 4-8 weeks to develop, while more complex sites can take several months or even longer. Additionally, ongoing maintenance and updates may also be required to keep the site running smoothly. Ultimately, the specific timeline for website development will depend on the individual project and should be discussed with the development team.
  • 3. What will you need from me?
    We will need the following information and materials from you to develop your website:
    1. A detailed description of the overall design and layout you envision for your website.
    2. Any specific functionality or features you would like to include on the website, such as an online store, contact form, or blog.
    3. Any branding materials, such as logos and color schemes, that should be incorporated into the website design.
    4. Any existing content, such as text and images, that you would like to include on the website.
    5. Access to any necessary back-end systems, such as a CMS or CRM, that the website will need to integrate with.
    6. A timeline for when you would like the website to be completed and launched. "
  • 4. Who hosts the website?
    There are a few options for hosting your website, including:
    1. Shared hosting: This is a popular option for small and medium-sized websites. It involves sharing server resources with other websites.
    2. Dedicated hosting: This option is ideal for larger websites that require more resources and control. It involves renting an entire server for your website.
    3. Cloud hosting: This is a newer option that involves hosting your website on a network of servers. This allows for more flexibility and scalability.
    4. Managed hosting: This option is ideal for those who don't have the technical expertise to manage their own hosting. It involves a hosting provider managing your server and website for you.
    Ultimately, the best hosting option for you will depend on your website's size, traffic, and specific needs. It's recommended to research and compare different hosting providers to find the best fit for you.
  • 5. Will my website be mobile friendly?
    Yes, your website will be mobile and tablet friendly. We will ensure that the website is optimized for all devices, including smartphones and tablets, so that it is easy to navigate and view on any device. This includes responsive design, which adjusts the layout and content to fit the screen size of the device being used. Additionally, we will test the website on various devices to ensure proper functionality.
